Intelligent closed-loopbattery management driven by data





To solve the pain points in the industry, we have decided to develop a battery full-life cycle system, starting from the raw materials of the battery, using it as a power battery, and reusing it as an energy storage battery before recycling it. It is worth mentioning that the reason why such a system can achieve a closed-loop is that we first developed an advanced vehicle-mounted BMS, which can collect battery usage data while managing the battery. This provides data support for later battery recycling classification and reuse.

Solution

01

Intelligent prediction

Utilize advanced algorithms and data analysis techniques to accurately predict key indicators such as battery performance and lifespan, and provide accurate prediction results.

02

Intelligent warning

By monitoring battery data in real-time, potential safety hazards and faults can be quickly identified, and timely warning notices can be issued to ensure the safety of car owners and the stable operation of the battery.

03

Intelligent optimization

By comprehensively considering battery usage and user needs, automatically adjusting charging and discharging strategies, optimizing battery performance and lifespan, and improving energy utilization efficiency.

04

Intelligent planning

Based on vehicle driving routes, charging needs, etc., intelligently plan the location and time of charging stations to facilitate the travel of car owners.

05

Intelligent after-sales service

Through remote monitoring and diagnosis, timely detection of battery failures and issues, providing personalized after-sales service and support, and ensuring user satisfaction.

06

Intelligent restructuring

Use Big data analysis and intelligent algorithms to optimize the configuration and combination of batteries to improve overall performance and energy efficiency.
